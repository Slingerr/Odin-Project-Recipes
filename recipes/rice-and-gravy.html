Chicken-Andouille Gumbo

Description

This Chicken-Andouille Gumbo recipe was pulled from a hand-me down set of recipes that came from my grandparents. This recipe calls for making the roux from scratch, and is most aligned with Central Louisiana's Cajun cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Stewing Chicken
  • 1 lb.Aandouille, sliced 1/4 in.
  • 6 large Onions (chopped)
  • 1 small bunch Green Onions (minced)
  • 1 tbsp. Celery (chopped)
  • 1 clove Garlic (chopped)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Cayenne Pepper
  • 3/4 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Cooking Oil
  • 6 cup hot Water
  • 1 tbsp. Parsely (chopped fine)

Steps

  1. Cut up chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat 1 cup oil in heavy skillet and fry chicken until brown.
  3. Remove chicken an dput aside
  4. Pour reamining oil into large heavy pot ot make roux.
  5. After roux is made, lower heat and add all chopped ingredients, except green onions, garlic, and parsley. .
  6. Cover and simmer until onions are clear.
  7. Add sliced andouille and chicken. Cover and let simmer half an hour, stirring often.
  8. Add water, garlic, parsley, and green onions
  9. Increase heat until mixture boils.
  10. Lower heat, cover and cook for about 1 hour 30mins to 2 hours.
  11. Serve over rice.
