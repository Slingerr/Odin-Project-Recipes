Pecan Pralines

Description

Pecan praline candy is a southern favorite, and this recipe is good for making about 35 to 40 pieces at a time.

Ingredient

  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 1 cup PET Milk (evaporated)
  • 1 stick Butter
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1 cup Pecan Halves

Steps

  1. Combine the sugars, salt, and evaporated milk.
  2. Stir over low heat until the sugar dissolves. Stirring constantly so the mixture does not burn.
  3. Cook to "soft ball" stage (about 15 minutes) then cool slightly.
  4. Add butter, vanilla, and pecans then beat until creamy.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto a piece of waxed paper.
