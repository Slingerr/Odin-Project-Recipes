Seafood Jamabalaya

Description

This recipe is for a seafood based jamabalaya.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. Shrimp (peeled)
  • 1 lb. Crabmeat
  • 1 doz. Oysters
  • 1 lb. Smoked Sausage (sliced)
  • 3 cups Rice
  • 1 small can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 lg. Onion (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup Parsley (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Boil rice and set aside.
  2. Saute onion over medium-low heat in oil until almost brown
  3. Add shrimp and cook 15 minutes - stirring occasionally
  4. Add parsely, shallots, and bell pepper; saute 10 minutes, stirring.
  5. Add smoked sausage and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add 3 cups water and cook for 30 minutes over medium heat.
  7. Add oysters and crabmeat; cook 15 minutes
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add rice little at a time, steadily stirring, until all rice is in.
  10. Heat over medium heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
